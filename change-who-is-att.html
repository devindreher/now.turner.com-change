Who is AT&T

AT&T is a provider of wireless, next-gen TV, and high speed internet services. The company's services and products are marketed under the AT&T, Cricket, and DIRECTV brands.

On this page, you can find out more about AT&T.

Company Strategy

Entertainment & OTT

  • Content on any device, anytime, any place. Create a seamless mobile and broadband experience where customers access their entertainment content on any device, enabled by AT&T's mobile and broadband networks.
  • Bring three Fortune 100 entities, DIRECTV, AT&T Wireless, and legacy AT&T internet and entertainment business, under one roof.
  • Create an unparalleled experience for customers who subscribe to both DIRECTV and AT&T Wireless — the consumption of DIRECTV content will not contribute towards mobile monthly data allowance ("zero rating").
  • DIRECTV Now — as nearly 60% of AT&T wireless traffic is video, DIRECTV Now sets out to be the "aggregator of aggregators" with a breadth of content and exclusive offerings.

Culture & Workforce Disruption

  • To help the transition from legacy telecom to today's IP and broadband focused marketplace, AT&T has targeted 150K employees for retraining, who will complete more than two million courses in order to reposition their skills.
  • In 2016, ~50% of AT&T's workforce had backgrounds in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) with the goal of 90% of jobs in 2020 requiring STEM capability in the technology organization.
  • To achieve the "reskilling" goal, AT&T has implemented programs such as Learning Portal for training courses, Skills Coaches for personalized guidance,apprenticeships for real-world experiences in next-gen job opportunities, and hiring processes for hiring the right skill sets, including adding reskilled employees to the candidate pool.

Innovation

  • AT&T Foundries — in-house think tanks, which act as innovation engines, injecting new ideas and thought processes into the larger AT&T culture.
  • The AT&T Foundries operate in six facilities throughout the world; in 2016 the Company opened a facility in Houston focusng on connected healthcare applications.
