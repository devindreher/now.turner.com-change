The Ending Zone: Saying Goodbye to the Current State

Every change begins with saying goodbye. During this time, we often feel loss and begin to "let go" of the way things were. To help yourself or your team through this period, use the tools below. Remember — it's hard to start the new before you let go of the old.



Personal Impact Analysis

This tool helps individuals identify and assess their personal losses so you can develop a strategy to manage your journey through change.

DROP Worksheet

This individual exercise helps you dismantle, redefine, organize, and prepare so you can begin to move forward on your journey.

Team Impact Analysis

This tool helps leaders identify and assess their team's loss and begin to take actions to help deal with the losses around change.

Team Strategies

This leader exercise helps you begin to strategize and identify actions you can take to lead your team through change.
