How will this change impact you & your team?

Change is coming, but how much will it impact you and your team? This handy quiz will help you determine specific areas that this change will impact.



Instructions:

Answer each question from the perspective of your team and their experience with the concept of this change. Think of only your team, not your entire organization.

How satisfied are the employees with the current state of the business?
(1 = Satisfied // 5 = Dissatisfied)

 

 

How have employees viewed past changes?
(1 = Positively // 5 = Negatively)

 

 

Do you have any changes going on now besides this one?
(1 = Very Few Underway // 5 = Everything is Changing!)

 

 

How well were your past changes managed?
(1 = Well-Managed // 5 = Poorly Managed)

 

 

Is there a shared vision and direction for the organization?
(1 = Shared & Unified // 5 = Different & Shifting)

 

 

How open are people to new ideas & change?
(1 = Open & Receptive // 5 = Closed & Resistant)

 

 

What are employees rewarded for?
(1 = Embracing Change & Risk-Taking // 5 = Consistency & Predictability)

 

 

How is leadership power distributed?
(1 = Centralized // 5 = Distributed)

 

 

How do senior leaders demonstrate change competency?
(1 = Effectively // 5 = Lack Skills & Knowledge)

 

 

How do managers demonstrate change competecy?
(1 = Effectively // 5 = Lack Skills & Knowledge)

 

 

How well do employees manage change?
(1 = Very Well // 5 = Poorly)
