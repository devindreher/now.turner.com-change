Assess Your Change Challenges

Complete this assessment by ranking the statements by how much you strongly agree (5) or strongly disagree (1) with the statement. Drag the sliders (or tap, if you're on mobile) to the number that best represents your response.

 

Awareness Of the Need For Change

I understand the business reason for change.


I understand the risks of not changing.


I understand the impact the change has on my day-to-day work activities.

Desire to Support & Participate In the Change

I am personally motivated to be a part of the change.


I look forward to the new, changed environment.


My peers support the change.

Knowledge of How to Change

I have the skills and knowledge to be successful during the change.


I have the skills and knowledge to be successful after the change.


Training has been adequate to prepare me for the environment after the change.

Ability to Implement Required Skills & Behaviors

I have the ability to perform the new duties required by the change.


I can get support when I have problems and questions.


I have practice at performing in the new environment.

Reinforcement & Sustainment of the Change

The organization is committed to keeping the change in place.


I know the consequences of not performing new activities.


I am rewarded for performing in the new way.

Now that you've responded to those questions, take a moment to reflect.


Which areas of change were the lowest for you?
Those areas represent where you might require additional support.


Which areas of change were the highest for you?
These can be considered where you are currently strong. How might you leverage those strengths to help support your leadership or your peers?


Finally, after looking at your responses, what kind of support could you ask from your leader? Make a list of these requests and plan to talk about them with your leader at your next 1:1 meeting.
