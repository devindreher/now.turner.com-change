Rock the Change

There's one thing that's constant — change. In Rock the Change, we'll explore how to ensure balance and focus during periods of change, learn to influence up, and how to provide support to your team.


Rock the Change is for those who want to act as leaders through periods of change — whether that leadership is formal (by having direct reports) or informal.

You Will:
  • Understand the implications of change initiatives on people and your role in guiding employees through change transitions.
  • Identify what's in/out of control in change and how to influence up to support it.
  • Apply change leadership tools and concepts in our environment.
