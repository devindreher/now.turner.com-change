The time is now.

Media companies are embracing a new reality as we charge into the future. Advancements in technology have caused consumers to have and want more ways to consume and engage with compelling content. This has a radical influence on programming, advertising and marketing, and consolidation that cannot be ignored.


These changes in our industry and our fans' viewing behaviors shape the way we think about content — how we produce, distribute, and monetize it. There are opportunities to seize admist all this change.


That's where you come in. We need your help to create, foster, and drive Turner to be an organization that keeps us at the top.


As a leader, you deal with large and small changes in organizations and people every day. As an employee, you also deal with lots of change in your work, teams, roles, or processes. But, change is hard. Sometimes even the best of us get stuck.


These pages give you simple tools and other techniques to leverage when leading change for your teams. And, if you still need help, there are colleagues here to assist.
