The Neutral Zone: Where Am I?

The heart of change is often the hardest. You aren't doing what you used to do, but you aren't doing what you will be doing. People often feel confused and frustrated during this time wondering, "Where am I?" Others may feel a surge of creativity and possibilities to shape "the new."

Below are tools that will help you gain understanding, build agility, and encourage innovation during this challenging phase of change.



CUSP Worksheet

This tool helps individuals begin to reestablish control, understanding, support, and purpose during the heart of change.

Team VUCA Activity

A simple approach to help teams or individuals navigate constant or unpredictable change.

Force Field Team Activity

Use this approach with your team to visualize forces in favor and against a change and create a strategy to support the change.

Managing Disruption

A guide for individuals and leaders to manage disruption and conquer challenges throughout change.

Communicating for Clarity

This handout will help individuals ask questions that will increase their understanding and help leaders communicate effectively.

Temporary Solutions Team Activity

This tool will help you and your team begin to build a bridge between the old and the new.
